Global Coronavirus cases top 25 million

WASHINGTON: The number of confirmed coronavirus cases globally has topped 25 million. The US leads the count with 5.9 million cases,followed by Brazil with 3.8 million and India with 3.5 million. The real number of people infected by the virus around the world is believed to be much higher-perhaps 10 times higher in the US, according to the Centre for Disease Control and Prevention- given testing limitations and the many mild cases that have gone unreported or unrecognised.
